History

Boston was founded in 1630 by English Puritans and was considered the birthplace of the American Revolution. Samuel Adams led the Sons of Liberty and inspired other colonists to fight for their freedom against the British. Other significant events that occurred in Boston include the Boston Tea Party, Boston Marathon Bombing, etc. Today boston has become known as the College Town and countless people come to Boston every year.
