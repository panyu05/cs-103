About Halloween

Halloween is a holiday occurring on the 31st of October every year rooted in the ancient Celtic festival of Samhain. People wear costumes to ward off ghosts while celebrating the harvest and end of summer. Now Halloween has become a holiday were people dress up for fun and decorate to scare each other and go around trick or treating for candy. Its popularity has grown so much that October has become known as the Spooky Season.
