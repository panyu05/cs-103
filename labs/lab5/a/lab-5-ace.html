Fried Rice

Specifically Chinese fried rice is my favorite food because it is comforting and simple to make.

Ingredients

  • soy sauce
  • 2 cups of white rice
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 strips of bacon

Preparation

  1. Heat up a pan to medium high with small pieces of bacon
  2. Remove bacon once it is cooked and add in the 2 eggs
  3. Once the eggs are cooked add in 2 cups of white rice and the cooked bacon
  4. Add in soy sauce and oyster sauce to taste
  5. Serve and enjoy
